My Notes
What You Get

After downloading this application you can expect total privacy of you data. You will find biometric authentication support (on eligible devices) which enhances security. We are striving to add more functionalities such as the ability to pin notes so your most important notes remain at the top, rich text support, and many more.

What You Pay

This is the best part! You pay NOTHING! My Notes is a completely free application, and yes, all the updates in the future are also going to be free. You will not be pestered by any annoying ads either as showing any ads might compromise your data.
